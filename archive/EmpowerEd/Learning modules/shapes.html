Learn About Shapes

Circle

A circle is a round shape with no corners. All points on the edge are the same distance from the center.

Fun Fact: The circumference of a circle is calculated as 2πr!

Square

A square has four equal sides and four right angles. All sides are the same length.

Fun Fact: The area of a square is calculated as side²!

Triangle

A triangle has three sides and three corners. The sum of the angles in a triangle is always 180 degrees.

Fun Fact: Triangles are the building blocks of geometry!

Rectangle

A rectangle has four sides, with opposite sides being equal in length. It also has four right angles.

Fun Fact: The area of a rectangle is calculated as length × width!

Hexagon

A hexagon has six sides and six corners. The interior angles of a hexagon add up to 720 degrees.

Fun Fact: Honeycombs are made up of hexagonal shapes!
